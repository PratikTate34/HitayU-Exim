About HitayU Exim

HitayU Exim is a growing export-import company from Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad), Maharashtra, India.

Our journey began with premium quality A4 Printing Papers, Tissue Papers, Turmeric Powder and Red Chilli Powder. Today we proudly export stationery products and agricultural products to international markets while maintaining the highest quality standards.
